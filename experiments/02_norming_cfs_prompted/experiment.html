On the following screens you'll see pairs of characters in certain places where one of them asks a question.

The other person responds based on what they are seeing.

Your task is to guess which thing the responding person is seeing. You do this by distributing ten coins across the options offered in the bottom half of the screen.

The more coins you give an option, the more likely you think that was the option the person responding to the question was looking at.
